🦛 The Hippopotamus and the Little Fish 🐠

Once upon a time, there was a big, kind hippopotamus named Henry. He lived in a beautiful blue lake in Africa. Henry was very lonely because he had no friends.

One sunny morning, Henry saw a little goldfish swimming near the shore. The fish looked sad and scared.

"Hello, little fish! What's your name?" Henry asked in a gentle voice.

"My name is Goldie," the fish said softly. "I'm lost and I can't find my family."

Henry felt sorry for Goldie and wanted to help. They swam together on the lake and finally found a family of fish among the green algae.

Since then, Henry and Goldie have become best friends. Henry never felt lonely again. ❤️
